onTrackStuck

fun onTrackStuck(player: AudioPlayer, track: AudioTrack, thresholdMs: Long): Unit

Fired by Lavaplayer when the track that was playing got stuck.

This sends a TRACK_STUCK event, wrapped in a Dispatch Response, across a WebSocketChannel.

Parameters

player - The AudioPlayer object itself.

track - The AudioTrack that got stuck.

thresholdMs - The threshold the track was/is stuck for.
