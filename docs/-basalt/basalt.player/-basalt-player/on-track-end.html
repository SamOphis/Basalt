onTrackEnd

fun onTrackEnd(player: AudioPlayer, track: AudioTrack, endReason: AudioTrackEndReason): Unit

Fired by Lavaplayer when an AudioTrack ends.

This sends a TRACK_ENDED event, wrapped in a Dispatch Response, across a WebSocketChannel. It also cancels the previously-started update task and sets it to null.

Parameters

player - The AudioPlayer object itself.

track - The AudioTrack that was ended.

endReason - The reason why the track ended.
